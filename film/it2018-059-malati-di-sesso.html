Synopsis

Giacomo and Giovanna meet one day in the study of a psychoanalyst, where they both go looking for a solution to the problem that makes them similar. They will be overwhelmed by a series of misadventures that will lead them around Italy, from Rome to the Southern Apulia, and finally to Valle D'Aosta and Mont Blanc. They will face the most surreal and irrepressible situations. Everything is intertwined with the lifes of their best friends, Livio, an inveterate player and Eleonora, a convinced animal rights activist.
