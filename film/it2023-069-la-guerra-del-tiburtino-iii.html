La guerra del Tiburtino III

2023

Rome - Strange tales from the block

A small meteorite falls from on the sky on the Tiburtino III neighborhood, in the extreme suburbs of Rome, and is picked up by Leonardo De Sanctis, the father of a local drug dealer Pinna. In the following days, almost all the inhabitants begin to behave strangely and to raise barricades around their territory. Pinna decides to investigate the phenomenon together with his friends and Lavinia Conte, a famous fashion blogger who has found herself trapped in the neighborhood. The aliens, starting from Tiburtino III, are willing to conquer the world. To Pinna and his improbable group, the task of saving it.
