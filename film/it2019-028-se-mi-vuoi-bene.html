Se mi vuoi bene

2019

Diego (Claudio Bisio) is a successful lawyer, but he’s also chronically depressed. One day he meets Massimiliano (Sergio Rubini), the owner of an eccentric shop, Chatters, that sells nothing but conversations. And it is just chatting with him that Diego understands the solution to get out of his emotional swamp: to do good to all his loved ones. Diego surgically identifies the problems that he believes afflict his mother, his father, his brother, his daughter, his friends, even his ex-wife and, with the precision of a blindfolded sniper, he ends up ruining the existence of each of them. But not all is lost...
