Hunter from Elsewhere - a Journey with Helen Britton

Filmmaker Elena Alvarez Lutz traces the career of artist Helen Britton, interviews companions, and accompanies Britton on her travels. The artist is shown in her studio in Munich, during exhibition preparations, and in doing research on old traditions of jewelry. This gradually creates an impression of Britton's creative process and her artistic motivation.
