Tierras Construidas

2019

At the end of the 60's a group of artists decides to settle in a village in deep Spain and turn it into a cultural center. After the death of all their companions, only Felix is left there. His life spans a post-war childhood, a bohemian and resistant youth, and a maturity of recognition, when all his generation mates have disappeared. The film reflects the painter's life during one year, showing the seasonal changes in the landscape as well as the artistic and creative evolution of the protagonist.
