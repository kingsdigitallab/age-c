Io, noi e Gaber

2023

Gaber, us and I

Filmed in Milan and Viareggio, at Giorgio Gaber’s haunts, the film shows the private side of the performer, in the words of his daughter and those close to him over the years; yet it is also an ensemble film that contains the great personalities and artists who worked with and adored Gaber throughout his life.
Genre
Documentary – Participatory mode, Biopic
