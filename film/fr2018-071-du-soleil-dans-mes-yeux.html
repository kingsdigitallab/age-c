Du soleil dans mes yeux

2018

Irene came out unscathed of an accident, where her husband was killed. She was pregnant and decided she would keep the baby, but it was hard to overcome the tragedy. She gave up her career as a stage actress, and her child, Emile, was put in care of a nurse called Helene. Now Irene seems to be able to raise her boy, and she meets him in La Rochelle, where she makes a new acquaintance: Yann, a sailor. It is love at first sight.
