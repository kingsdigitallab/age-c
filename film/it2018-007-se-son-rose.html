Se son rose

2018

Leonardo is a 50 year old single who is a successful journalist on the web, dealing with high technology and has a 15 year old daughter, Yolanda, the legacy of a shipwrecked marriage. Yolanda is tired of seeing her father feed on frozen spring rolls and bask in his regressive infantilism, and thinks that the keystone can be a stable relationship. To put him in front of his countless failures in sentimental matters, Yolanda decides to send to all the former Leonardo's girlfriends a text message that says: "I have changed, let's try again!". And his exes respond, each according to his own mode.
Genre
