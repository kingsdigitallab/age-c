La settima onda

2018

Against the wonderful background of sea, sun barren lands and Mediterrenean flavors, two men, whose two lives profoundly differ from each other are destined to meet. Their stories intertwine in a strange but intense bond, which is simply born and grows, though tainted by past and present sufferings, dormant and never soothed. It is the story of Tanino, a young fisherman from the south, and passionate dreamer, driven daily by family events and personal misfortunes. One day his meeting with a certain someone leads him to discover he shares an unexpected and deep love for something that ends up making them very similar in their diversity.
