Noi ce la siamo cavata

2023

In most parts of the world, school is a crucial developmental environment in which to grow, form and find self-actualization. Then there are some places where school saves your life. The mission of the nonprofit organization Still I Rise is to solve the global school crisis by emancipating the most discriminated against, poor and vulnerable children through a cutting-edge educational model that aims to provide excellent education, free of charge, to all the children in the world.
