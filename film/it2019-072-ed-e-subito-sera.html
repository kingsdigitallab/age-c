In the background of a small drug-dealing square at the foot of the Vesuvius, there are dreams and hopes of three families that come from different social origins. The one of Dario, which is honest and industrious who wants to renovate the driving schools owned by his family. The one of the magistrate De Martino, who would like to eradicate the cancer of the Camorra. And more so, the family of Moccuso, a cowardly boss on the rise.
