Si muore tutti democristiani

2018

The film recounts the work path of three friends and colleagues: the ethical, politicized and anti-systemic past, the present precarious and ultimately the compromise that brings economic serenity but which extinguishes their young passions, in the backdrop the economical and social Italian crisis.
