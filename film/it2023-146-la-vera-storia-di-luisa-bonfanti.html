La vera storia di Luisa Bonfanti

2023

Luisa Bonfanti is an actress, who worked in the '70, among others, with directors Maselli, Scola, Petri, Maselli, Lizzani, Ferrara, Montaldo. She commits suicide in 1984, when she is 36, after devoting herself to x-rated movies. A fake woman, but a true portrait of a certain age of Italian cinema and society.
