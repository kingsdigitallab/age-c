Fake Criminals

Riccardo, Amir, Pietro, and Massimo are four inseparable friends who work together as security guards. They're not particularly brilliant nor brave. When Amir loses his job, his friends feel compelled to help him provide for his large family, and they start piling up bad ideas. Eventually turning into criminal masterminds, their plan will lead to a whirlwind of events, encounters, and dangerous adventures with a few surprises. However, our four heroes keep more than one ace up their sleeve.
