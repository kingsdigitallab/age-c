Yohen: The Universe in a Bowl

Jean Girel, a renowned French ceramist, particularly famous for his work inspired in the tradition of the Song ceramics, has taken up a new challenge: to unravel the ancient mystery of the magnificent « yohen temmoku » tea bowls, of which only three intact pieces exist today.
