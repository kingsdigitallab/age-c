Synopsis (original)

Isabel, que nunca ha recibido muchas muestras de cariño y comprensión de su marido Sebastián, militar prejubilado, sufre un brote de Alzheimer. Empieza entonces a confundir a su esposo con otras personas, y es así, por accidente, que Sebastián descubre la verdad que nunca reconoció sobre su agrio carácter.

Synopsis

Isabel does not live her best love life. Sebastián, a pre-retired military man and her husband, has never shown him affection or understanding. One day Isabel suffers an Alzheimer's outbreak and then she begins to confuse her husband with other people, and as a result, Sebastián realizes the truth that he never recognized about his sour character.
