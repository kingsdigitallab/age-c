Synopsis (original)

Actors Micki and Ginger, a broke and unemployed couple, team up with Micki's brother Reno for a criminal coup: They kidnap Lilli, the daughter of a rich family, and demand 50.000 € ransom money. At first, everything seems to go according to plan, but then one of the three kidnappers gets qualms of conscience. Meanwhile, Lilli's three siblings go on a search for their missing sister, which leads them to several encounters with strange characters and eventually to a dark family secret.
