Synopsis (original)

Este documental es un retrato de los cien años de historia del Partido Comunista (PCE) pero, sobre todo, es un homenaje a los parias de la tierra, los oprimidos y olvidados, quienes dieron su vida por la libertad y la democracia.

Synopsis

This documentary is a portrait of the 100-year history of the Communist Party of Spain but, above all, it is a tribute to the wretched of the earth, the oppressed and forgotten, those who gave their lives for freedom and democracy.
