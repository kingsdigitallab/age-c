Zwei Herren im Anzug

2018

Two Men in Suits

A story that spans 100 years in Germany, it is a domestic epic about three generations of lakeside landlords, dealing with war and destruction, old power and new wealth, and the so-called "Years of Plenty."
Genre
War Film, Drama, Historical film (Heritage Film)
