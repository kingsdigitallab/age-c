Tequila. Sexo, drogas y rock and roll

2022

"Tequila. Sexo, Drogas y Rock and Roll" is a documentary that tells the story of the legendary Spanish rock band who provided the soundtrack for the liberation of young people during The Spanish Transition between the late 70s to the early 80s. Five young people whose turned in the Spanish Rolling Stones whose suddenly pull off their dream of making good music but that they were destroyed by the incredible fame and success they reached. Although their songs have succeeded in lasting over time as indestructible classics for the later generations as well as new legions of fans.
