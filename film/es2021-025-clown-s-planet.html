Clown's planet

2021

Clowns bring us light relief; we don't take them seriously. Yet in this doc, director Hector Carre dives into the rarefied world of 'activist clowns' like the iconic Patch Adams. From refugee camps in the Holy land to hospitals and orphanages in Russia, this is a global journey into the healing powers of clowns on a mission. Can the combination of humour and the will to fight for justice make for an activist with added impact?
