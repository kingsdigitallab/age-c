Now is Now

A bride escapes from her wedding. Takes a taxi and tries to run away from her current artifical life. The taxi driver is not on duty that day, but takes her everywhere she wants. The bride tries to go back to the past, tries to change decisions she have made years before. The taxi driver tries to show her that the way out can only be the way ahead, not the way she left. Of course they have adventures, have excellent dialogues, they make us laugh and sometimes cry. A good mirror for the 30-40s generation and not only in Hungary.
Genre
Comedy, Road Movie, Romance
