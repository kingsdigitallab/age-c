Documentation about a number of criminals who committed their crimes due to alcohol or drug addiction and are now in the so-called penal system instead of a regular prison. This type of enforcement usually takes place in a forensic psychiatry clinic, where the criminals can move relatively freely and receive psychological support to overcome their addiction. In his film, director Sobo Swobodnik interviews numerous inmates of the clinic and has them tell him about their past, their goals and fears, wishes and dreams. In the brutally open-hearted conversations it becomes clear, among other things, that the therapy works differently for different offenders and that addiction is easier to overcome for some than for others
