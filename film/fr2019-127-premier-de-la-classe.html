Top of the Class

Abou, 13, is his father’s pride and joy. Unlike his 3 brothers, he’s « top of his class ». Well, that’s what he pretends to be. Actually, Abou is more the king of lies and rigged report cards ! When the first parent-teacher conference comes up, he will stage the biggest lie of his life : hire fake teachers to meet his father, while his real teachers will meet his fake father. It should be easy... in theory !
