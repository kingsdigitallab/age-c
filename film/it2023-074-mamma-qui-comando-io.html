Mamma qui comando io

2023

When, in a divorce case, a judge decides to award the family home to Francesco (9) and that the parents will take turns in the house every Monday for six months, the child interprets that he is now the boss. Listening on the one hand to the tips of his gang of friends and on the other to the advice of his grandfather, the boy will try to assume the consequences. While trying to reunite his parents, he will also discover love and his own 'butterflies in the stomach'. All sorts of bizarre and comical situations will unfold as the boy will make his own decisions. To begin with, he will try to scare his father's new girlfriend. And also, organize a wild party on social networks. He is now the boss.
