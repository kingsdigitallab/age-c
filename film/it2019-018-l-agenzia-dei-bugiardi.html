L'agenzia dei bugiardi

2019

Fred, Diego and Paolo are members of a brilliant, diabolic agency that provides alibis to their clients. Fred falls in love with Clio, a defender of truth and honesty and can't tell her about his job. Even worse, Fred discovers that Clio's father is a client of the agency, who called him to help him conceal from his wife his affair with his young mistress.
