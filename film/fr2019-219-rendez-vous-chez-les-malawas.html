Synopsis (original)

Pour la spéciale Noël de son émission phare Rencontre au bout du bout du monde, Léo Poli emmène non pas un, mais quatre invités exceptionnels. Est-ce vraiment une bonne idée? Synopsis

For the special Christmas edition of his pioneering TV show “Extremely Extreme Encounters”, Léo Poli invites not one but four exceptional guests to an unknown, exotic destination. Is this really a good idea? Our stars set out to meet the Malawas, one of the most isolated tribes in the world. A comedy that takes us into the wilds of nature… and of human nature!
