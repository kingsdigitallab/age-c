Synopsis

Five elderly guests of the Opera Don Guanella retirement home in Rome, bored by daily life and disappointed by the separation of their nearest and dearest, decide to flee to Puglia to realise their life's dream: They are Rocco (Ivano Marescotti), a failed trader, his brother Luchino (Enzo Salvi), an obsessive compulsive cook, Antonio (Babak Karimi), a long-time ex-captain, Erica (Corinne Clery), a former heart patient singer, and Mirna (Sandra Milo), who arrived in Italy from Serbia at the outbreak of the Yugoslav wars. Enthusiastic about the adventure Mirna is about to embark on to find the much sought-after Dragomir (Antonio Catania), her old love believed dead but actually hidden for decades in Salento, the four join the journey. They are certain that their lives will change for the better once they meet the powerful criminal. Their personal stories will alternate between passion and adventure, in a Salento that will be the setting for their emotions. Will our friends succeed in realising their dreams?
