Pozzis, Samarcanda

2021

Cocco and Stefano, two unlikely friends linked by a single goal: from the mountains of Friuli, to reach Samarkand. Cocco is an old motorcyclist with a troubled past who dreams of heading east on his '39 Harley-Davidson. Stefano is a young director who wants to make a film about his friend's business and story. Amid the ups and downs of a quixotic journey, the challenge will prove greater than expected. Only the friendship between the two will allow both to reach their goal.
