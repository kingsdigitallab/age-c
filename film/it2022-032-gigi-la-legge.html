The adventures of Gigi the law

Gigi is a police officer in a countryside where nothing ever happens. One day, however, a young girl throws herself under a train. This is not the first time such a thing happens. Facing this unexplainable suicide wave, Gigi starts investigating a strange world, between reality and fantasy, where a garden turns into a jungle and where an ever-smiling policeman keeps his heart open to love.
