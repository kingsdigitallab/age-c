Rien ne destinait le braqueur, un rien anarchiste, à croiser la route de ce prof d’histoire sans histoires. S’engage alors un étrange rapport entre les deux hommes où se mêlent emprise et complicité. Synopsis

After a failed hold-up, an injured Antoine finds a hideout at Elias’: a shy and lonely professor. Then a strange and unexpected bound will form between the two men and the bank robber will help Elias to get out of his shell. But when Lucie, a young and ambitious cop, gets involved in the case, things get more twisted.
