Jazzfieber

2023

Jazz is all the rage in clubs in 2023 and is particularly popular with young people. But few people know the origins of this music, which conquered the dance scene in Germany 100 years ago. The film goes in search of traces in the German jazz capitals of Berlin, Frankfurt, Stuttgart and Munich and sheds light on how the music style came to Germany, why young people who fell for swing fever were sent to forced labor camps and why jazz musicians were deported to concentration camps.
