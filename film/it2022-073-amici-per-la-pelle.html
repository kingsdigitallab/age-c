Amici per la pelle

2022

During a holiday in the United States, the protagonist is seized by severe abdominal pain that forces him to return to Italy in a daring way and to be hospitalized urgently. The response of the doctors leaves no doubt: an immediate liver transplant is needed. It will be the beginning of an odyssey that will force a 20-year-old boy to make important decisions and temporarily give up his passions. However, it will be his will to live, combined with the deep bond with his parents, to allow him to face this difficult situation with a smile.
