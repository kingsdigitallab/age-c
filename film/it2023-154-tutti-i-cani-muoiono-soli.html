Synopsis (original)

Un padre mette in scena la storia di un incontro tra due solitudini, il ricongiungimento difficile tra un padre e una figlia, l'ultima occasione di un uomo alla deriva, un vecchio delinquente che ha più passato che futuro.

Synopsis

In a winter Sardinia, bruised and violent, the drama of Rudi takes place, a man in his sixties, a small suburban gangster who now wearily manages his business, until he has to take care of his daughter Susanna, alone and seriously ill. Rudi then abandons life on the streets and faces his limits, his fears, his impotence, he falls, gets up and finally understands that his daughter is the destiny that awaits him, the mission to be accomplished. But there is his world out there, the world of racketeering, debts, "odd jobs", which has not forgotten him.
