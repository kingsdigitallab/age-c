Essere Gigione - L'incredibile storia di Luigi Ciaravola

2018

Try an experiment. If possible, go to any Campanian town, Abruzzo, Molise, Basilicata, Lazio, and run the rumor that the next day in the central square the singer Laura Pausini will perform. Nobody will arrive. Now try this: refer to the same people that in a handful of hours there will be a concert of Gigione.
