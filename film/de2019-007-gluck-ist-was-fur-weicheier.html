Stefan Gabriel (MARTIN WUTTKE) is a lifeguard, committed father and always strives to see life positively. As he tries to give something back to society and engage as a death companion, his two daughters, twelve-year-old Jessica (ELLA FREY) and her older sister Sabrina (EMILIA BERNSDORF) struggle with their very own problems. Jessica is often mistaken for a boy and must constantly fight against her many strange ticks. Sabrina is seriously ill. Jessica would like to swap with her pretty sister who, despite illness, seems to have her life fully under control. The more Sabrina's health condition worsens, the worse Jessica's ticks become. A plan has to be done quickly. In an old book, the two encounter a special ritual meant to bring salvation. However, Sabrina and Jessica have to find a boy who sleeps with Sabrina. They have little time left, and Jessica does everything to put the life-saving plan into action, no matter how crazy he may be.
