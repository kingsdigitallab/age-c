La California

2022

Two young twins grow up in the Emilia province, expressing the two faces of an era and the adolescent nature that clashes with traditions. One of them will witness a murder and how a man of power is threatening the destruction of the ecosystem. Unite for the first time will fight to defeat a great danger to the community and to assert the best of themselves.
Genre
Thriller, Drama
