You Are Young You Are Pretty

Lucius Marnant is a retiree who feels that his life is three quarters behind him. Returning from a funeral, Lucius meets Lahire, a young man who proposes to improve his meager daily by participating in illegal clashes between old people. Very soon Lucius takes a liking to this new life, to money and to the forgetfulness of himself and of death.
