Wie Dich selbst?

2023

Nine people between 28 and 88 who couldn't be more different. Together they explore the meaning of self-love in their lives in an intimate conversation in the midst of Bavaria's nature with directors Ralph Gladitz and Susanne Petz and ask the question of the connection between love for oneself and love for one's neighbor.
