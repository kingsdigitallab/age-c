Una vita spericolata

2018

Reckless

When a young mechanic deep in debt has his bank loan rejected, a series of unfortunate events turns his rejection into a robbery. Along with his best friend, a former rally champion, and a miserable teen superstar as their hostage, they start on a crazy race across Italy. With police chases, bloodshed, and money spills, their escape is followed on social media, transforming them into heroes in this madcap Robin Hood road movie.
Genre
Comedy, Crime
