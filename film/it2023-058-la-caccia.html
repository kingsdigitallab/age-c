The Hunt

Giorgio, Mattia, Silvia and Luca. At the death of their father, the foursome will have to come to terms with the murder of their mother at the hands of their father, and with financial woes. Therefore they finally accept a challenge launched by Luca: a hunting expedition in the woods, of the kind they used to go on as children. The one who catches the biggest prey will automatically win the right to keep the entire inheritance.
