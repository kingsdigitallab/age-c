Gaston gets hired as an intern in a successful startup whose motto is "make the useless useful". Of course, his going to the office with his cat, his seagull and his goldfish in a car of the 1920's powered by solar panels doesn't make things easier... risking to jeopardize the future of the company, will he be able to put his genius at work to save it?
