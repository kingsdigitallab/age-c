Les Blagues de Toto

2020

Toto

Back-to-school! For troublemaker Toto, it’s the best playground for fooling around in front of his friends. But when a sculpture collapses during the opening of a museum, Toto is immediately blamed. The boy has only a few days to escape his penalty: boarding school. Toto has no other option but making a pact with Igor, the school nerd. Igor will help Toto prove his innocence and Toto will help Igor being cool. The unlikely duo turns into little detectives. A fun and exciting adventure begins…
Genre
Comedy, Children's Film
