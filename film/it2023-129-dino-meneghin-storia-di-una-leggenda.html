Synopsis

Meneghin. History of the greatest Italian basketball player of all time is a documentary that aims to fill an important gap in the horizon of sports stories: to make a product entirely dedicated to the career of Italy's greatest basketball player. Dino Meneghin is the emblem of our basketball: no other champion of a team sport has been, in the collective imagination, assimilated to a discipline like him. A true national icon, capable of building a bridge between past and present, bringing new generations of fans, but also the merely curious, closer to a legendary era of Italian basketball. Dino Meneghin, from the small camps of the Belluno province to the entrance by right into the history of world basketball.
