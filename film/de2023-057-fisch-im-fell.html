Fisch im Fell

2023

Six teenagers have created their own little meeting place in a remote hut in the forest. After a fire, however, the hideout is completely destroyed. But the catastrophe also has something good: it uncovers a time capsule containing mysterious coordinates. The friends want to find out what is behind them and set off for the South Tyrolean mountains against their parents' wishes. An adventurous journey awaits the friends, which puts the group's friendship and trust to the test.
