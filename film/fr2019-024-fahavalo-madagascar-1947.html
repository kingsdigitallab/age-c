Synopsis (original)

À Madagascar en 1947, les rebelles insurgés contre le système colonial sont appelés fahavalo, «ennemis» de la France. Les derniers témoins évoquent leurs longs mois de résistance dans la forêt, armés seulement de sagaies et de talismans.

Synopsis

They were called fahavalo – enemies - because they rebelled in 1947 against French colonial authorities in Madagascar. Today, filmmaker Marie-Clémence Andriamonta-Paes takes us where the events took place, on a journey to meet the last witnesses. They tell us about their fight for independence and their long months of resistance in the jungle, armed only with spears and talismans.
