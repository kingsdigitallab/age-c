Synopsis (original)

Pour son dernier jour avant la retraite, Dominique, une sage-femme au caractère bien trempé, est forcée de coopérer avec un jeune obstétricien arrogant. Ensemble, ils vont devoir accompagner cinq femmes à accoucher. Elles s’en souviendront toute leur vie…

Synopsis

Labor day is more about what happens before! Before giving life. It’s about crossing the entire country to be with your wife in time to hear your baby’s first cry, about saving an international crisis in between two contractions, about finding the strength to put aside your father’s death when your wife has decided to go through with giving birth in a Jacuzzi, or when you steal the cellphone of your patient to find out who the father is. All of this makes for a very special labor day.
