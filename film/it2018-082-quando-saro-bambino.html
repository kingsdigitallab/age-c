Once I Will Be Child

Six stories to recount a fundamental theme: childhood. Six stories, set in six different decades, from the years ' 50 to date, which bind each other through a common thread that will be revealed in the last episode, when Claudio, a 14-year-old aspiring filmmaker, decides to ask his relatives to tell him the moment they realized they were growing up. These are our stories: a championship game to be won, a small family mystery to solve, a special mission to fulfill, some advice to follow, a secret to confess. Six stories told with tenderness and sensitivity needed to involve everyone, young and old.
