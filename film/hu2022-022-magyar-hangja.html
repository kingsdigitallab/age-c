Synopsis

The Art of Dubbing... is a feature-length documentary about the history, past, present and possible future of Hungarian dubbing. The film explores how dubbing and some emblematic Hungarian dubbed versions became part of Hungarian cultural heritage and assets as well as the difficulties faced by professionals in the digital transition and the challenges of dubbing in the past and present. Important dubbing projects and dubbing roles are discussed, with a special focus on dubbing animated films, from Hungarian folk tales to South Park and Family Guy. The Art of Dubbing... provides a comprehensive overview of the profession, with dozens of well-known actors and professionals talking about the world of dubbing.
