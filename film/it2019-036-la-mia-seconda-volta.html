La mia seconda volta

2019

A Second Chance

Eighteen years old, vital and creative, a talent yet to be addressed: this is Giorgia (Mariachiara Di Mitri). She burns with desire to finish the Art High school and escape from the province where she grew up ... the same one where Ludovica (Aurora Ruffino) has settled to study at the Academy of Fine Arts. She's twenty-three and already knows what she wants: to become a set designer. Getting to know each other through Giorgia's brother (Simone Riccioni), they will discover the unpredictability of destiny, between deep friendships, unexpected loves and dreams of escape ignited by a colorful caravan on the banks of a lake. They will find courage to face the consequences of their choices ... because no man is an island and everyone's actions, for better or for worse, reflect on everyone else. A journey into adulthood that touches the hopes, fears, difficulties and joys of each path of growth.
