Locked up in a Nevada prison, Roman no longer has any contact with the outside world nor with his daughter. He's withdrawn into himself, with his anger and violence as his only company. To try to exit from his isolation, he joins a prisoners' rehabilitation program that involves taming wild horses. Alongside these mustangs as unpredictable as he is, Roman will gradually learn to control himself and to get over his past.
