Corro da te

2022

Run to you

Womanizer Gianni takes the art of seduction so far as to fake a disability in order to charm sexy Alessia. His idea is to get on a wheelchair and pity her into going on a date with him - as pity is the only emotion one can feel towards a disabled person, he reckons. Everything changes when he meets Chiara, a joyful, dynamic musician with a passion for tennis, despite her being paraplegic due to a car accident. Much to his surprise, the feelings Gianni has towards Chiara are in fact far removed from being pitiful.
