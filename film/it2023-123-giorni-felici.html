Synopsis

Margherita is an internationally renowned actress whose career is full of successes and prizes. She lives in Rome, and on the eve of a journey to Los Angeles, is struck by physical problems and diagnosed with amyotrophic lateral sclerosis (ALS). Antonio, her ex-companion, offers to help her and the two of them, separated for many years, reconstruct their relationship through suffering.
