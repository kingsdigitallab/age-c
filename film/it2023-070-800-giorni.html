800 Giorni

2010

January 1988. Upon returning from a party, Paolo Pierobon, son of a well-known Vicenza businessman, is kidnapped outside his home by exponents of the Ndrangheta. This nightmare will last more than 800 days spent living inside cramped and claustrophobic spaces. Difficult times that will bring him face to face with his disillusioned hopes and humiliation.
Genre
Crime, Drama, Historical film (Heritage Film)
