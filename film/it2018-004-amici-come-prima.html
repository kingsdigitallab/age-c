Amici come prima

2018

Cesare Proietti is the esteemed manager of the Relais Colombo, a luxury hotel in Milan. When a group of new Chinese business partners want to revolutionize everything, the hotel owner's daughter Luciana, decides to fire Cesare. When Cesare discovers that Luciana is seeking a caregiver to look after her father, he decides to dress up as a charming woman to apply for the job.
