The Accidental Detective

Giulia and Pietro are inseparable. Together with their exuberant group of friends they meet every night to comment on the events that have taken place nearby. One day, however, Pietro notices Marta, a girl with drug problems persecuted by people who blackmail her for money. He grows fond of her, triggering Giulia's jealousy who begins to be worried that her friend is getting into trouble.
