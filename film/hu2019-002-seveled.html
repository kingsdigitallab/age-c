Neither with You

Neither with you is a real romantic comedy about Gergo, a Hugh Grant-like intellectual man whose mother is having a serious heart disease and has only days left, and therefore she is obsessed with having a grandchild. To make his moms last hours happy, Gergo asks his ex-stripper, five-month-old pregnant neighbor, Saci to visit his mother in the hospital with him, and tell her that she is carrying his child under her heart. It's nice and good, but a new heart arrives, and mom is being treated and healing. Therefore Gergo and Saci are forced to play the role of the love couple. The avalanche starts: with the growing tendency of events, after a while, not only the dear mom, but her elderly friends and even Gergos colleagues think that Gergo and Saci are a couple with a soon to be born baby.
