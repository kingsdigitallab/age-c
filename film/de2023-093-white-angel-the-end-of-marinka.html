White Angel - The End of Marinka

Documentary about the evacuation of the small town of Marinka in Donetsk in eastern Ukraine, which was completely destroyed by Russian soldiers in 2022. The film accompanies the former Ukrainian policeman Vasyl, who during the war began to drive an ambulance marked "White Angel" on evacuation missions in his bombed-out hometown. His goal is to save residents from further bombings, often in vain. With a helmet camera, he documents the suffering that Russian aggression has brought to the Ukrainian people and to his hometown.
